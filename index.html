Understanding the trade-offs of the incremental approach

‚úÖ Advantages

  • Early delivery of working software to the client
  • Flexible ‚Äî easy to accommodate requirement changes
  • Lower initial delivery cost
  • Easier to test and debug smaller modules
  • Reduced risk through iterative feedback
  • Client gets important functionality early

‚ùå Disadvantages

  • Requires good planning and design upfront
  • Total cost may be higher than waterfall
  • System architecture must be well-defined early
  • Integration issues between increments possible
  • Not ideal for small projects or unclear requirements
  • Requires experienced team for best results
